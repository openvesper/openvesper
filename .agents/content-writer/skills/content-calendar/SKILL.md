# Content Calendar

A calendar earns its place by reducing your weekly decision-making about what to publish.

## Intake (always)

- **Goal:** Audience growth, lead gen, authority building, community
- **Channels:** Blog, newsletter, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, podcast, Substack
- **Capacity:** Hours per week, who's writing
- **Audience:** Who, what level, what problems
- **Pillar topics:** 3-5 themes the user has authority in
- **Timeframe:** 1 week / 4 weeks / 12 weeks

## Pillar themes

Pick 3-5 themes the user keeps returning to. Each piece of content should map to one pillar.

Example for a Solana developer audience:
1. Anchor patterns & gotchas
2. Token-2022 deep dives
3. Compute unit optimization
4. Recent Solana news commentary
5. Career advice for Solana devs

## Channel matrix

For each pillar × channel, decide format:

| Pillar | Blog (1500w) | Newsletter (500w) | X thread (8 tweets) | YouTube (10min) | Podcast (45min) |
|--------|---|---|---|---|---|
| Anchor patterns | Long-form tutorial | TL;DR + 1 takeaway | Hot take | Live coding | Interview author |
| Token-2022 | Spec walkthrough | Code snippet of week | Visualized | Demo | — |

## Cadence guidance

| Channel | Realistic weekly cadence |
|---------|--------------------------|
| Blog | 1 post/week (≥1500 words) |
| Newsletter | 1/week, same day |
| X/Twitter | 1-2 threads/week + 5-10 single tweets |
| LinkedIn | 2-3 posts/week |
| Instagram/TikTok | 3-5 posts/week (visual) |
| YouTube | 1/week (consistency > quality early) |
| Podcast | Weekly or biweekly |

**Don't promise more than you can deliver.** A consistent 1/week beats a sporadic 3/week.

## Repurposing chain

One pillar piece → many derivatives:

```
Long blog post (1500w)
    ↓
Newsletter TL;DR (500w + link)
    ↓
X thread (8 tweets, key points)
    ↓
LinkedIn post (1 angle)
    ↓
Instagram carousel (visual highlights)
    ↓
YouTube short (1-2min)
```

One week's work, six channel touches.

## Hook patterns

For each piece, write the hook BEFORE writing the body:

### Educational hooks
- "Most people get [X] wrong. Here's why:"
- "I spent N hours debugging [Y]. Here's what I learned:"
- "[Common belief] is a myth. Here's what's actually true:"

### Story hooks
- "Last week, I [unusual situation]. Here's what happened:"
- "I almost [failure outcome]. Here's how I caught it:"

### Contrarian hooks
- "Everyone says [X]. They're wrong, and here's why:"
- "The advice you keep hearing about [Y] is bad. Try this instead:"

## Calendar template

```markdown
# Content Calendar — [Period]

## Pillar themes
1. [Theme] — [audience problem this solves]
2. ...

## Week 1 — [Theme of the week]

**Blog (Mon):** [Title]
- Hook: [first line]
- 3 key points: ...
- CTA: [newsletter / product / book a call]

**Newsletter (Wed):** [Subject line]
- TL;DR of blog
- 1 new insight not in blog

**X thread (Thu):** [Hook]
- 8 tweets riffing on blog points
- Visual: [if relevant]

**LinkedIn (Fri):** [Different angle on same week's theme]

## Week 2 — [Theme]
...

## Repurposing notes
- Blog → quote graphics for IG
- Newsletter → segment for cold outreach
- YouTube → podcast clip
```

## Rules

- **Theme by week**, not by day — easier to stay coherent
- **Newsletter > social** for audience ownership
- **Quality > quantity** — one great post a week beats 7 mediocre
- **Track what works** — note which posts drove signups/replies; do more of that
- **Reserve 20% for opportunistic content** — react to news, trends
