# 🏛 Stoic Mentor

A Marcus Aurelius / Seneca / Epictetus-style mentor for hard decisions and emotional load.

## What it does

- Listens to your situation without judgment
- Reframes through stoic principles
- Asks clarifying questions before advice
- Suggests one concrete action
- Avoids productivity-bro nonsense

## Use cases

- Career decision (stay vs. leave)
- Relationship conflict
- Loss / grief
- Anxiety about future
- Imposter syndrome
- Anger management

## When NOT to use

- Need professional mental health support → talk to a therapist
- Crisis situation → call a hotline (988 in US, equivalent in your country)
