# Investment Thesis Write-Up

The discipline: write the thesis BEFORE buying, not after.

## Template

```markdown
# [TICKER / TOKEN] Investment Thesis

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Price at thesis:** $X
**Time horizon:** [3 months / 1 year / 3 years]
**Position size at full conviction:** X% of portfolio

## 1. One-sentence thesis

"I believe [TICKER] will [outperform / reach $X / achieve Y] because [primary driver], and I'd change my mind if [invalidation event]."

## 2. The business / protocol

[3-5 sentences: what they do, who pays them, why]

## 3. Why now? (Why does this opportunity exist?)

- [Reason 1: e.g., market overreacting to short-term news]
- [Reason 2: e.g., underfollowed by analysts]
- [Reason 3: e.g., catalyst coming in 6 months]

If you can't articulate why others don't see this — you might be missing something.

## 4. Key metrics

| Metric | Current | 12mo target | Source |
|--------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Revenue | $X | $Y | [10-K, ER call] |
| Growth rate | X% | Y% | [calc] |
| Margin | X% | Y% | [10-Q] |
| (For crypto): TVL, fees, FDV | ... | ... | [DefiLlama] |

## 5. Bull case (~25% probability)

[What needs to be true for 3x return]

## 6. Base case (~50% probability)

[Most likely outcome at 12mo]

## 7. Bear case (~25% probability)

[What happens if I'm wrong]

## 8. Risks (ranked)

1. [Risk + probability + severity]
2. ...

## 9. What would change my mind?

- [Specific event 1] → reduce position by half
- [Specific event 2] → exit entirely

## 10. Position sizing

- Initial entry: X% of portfolio
- Add zone: Y% if price drops to $Z (only if thesis still intact)
- Max position: W% (never exceed regardless of conviction)

## 11. Catalyst calendar

- [Month] [event]
- ...

## 12. Sources

- [Link to filings, transcripts, on-chain dashboards, primary research]
```

## Rules

- **No vague claims.** "Strong growth" → "30% YoY revenue growth, last 4 quarters"
- **Cite primary sources** — 10-K, SEC filings, on-chain data, not Twitter rumors
- **Pre-commit to invalidation** — write down what would make you sell BEFORE owning it
- **Update quarterly** — re-read your thesis with fresh eyes

⚠️ NOT FINANCIAL ADVICE. DYOR.
